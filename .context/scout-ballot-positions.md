## COOK COUNTY SUBCIRCUIT COURTS

> About two-thirds of Circuit Court judgeships are elected within **geographic subcircuits**.  
> Cook County has **20 subcircuits**. Voters see only races for their own subcircuit.  
> **Term:** 6 years. **Parties Contested:** Partisan primary (mostly Democratic in Cook County subcircuits).  
> Only residents of a subcircuit can run for — or vote in — subcircuit races.
